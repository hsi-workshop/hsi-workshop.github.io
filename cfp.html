Topics

The workshop encourages submissions on innovative technologies and applications that leverage heuristic search for solving industrial problems. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, problems and data sets, techniques, methodologies, and applications of heuristic search in (or relevant to) industry.

  • AI planning
  • Automated algorithm design
  • A* search
  • Bioinformatics
  • Combinatorial optimization
  • Constraint satisfaction problems
  • Cloud computing
  • Digital maps
  • Evolutionary computation
  • Local search
  • Logistics
  • Meta-heuristic
  • Network optimization
  • Resource allocation
  • Scheduling

Submission details

All papers should be typeset in the IJCAI style. Authors may submit long papers (no more than 6 pages plus one page of references) or short papers (no more than 2 pages plus up to one page of references). Contributed papers may also include presentations of related work previously presented in other conferences.

Papers must be submitted in PDF format via the CMT system. All submissions will go through a double-blind reviewing process, and thus the submissions should be anonymized.

Papers submitted to the workshop will be reviewed by at least two members of the program committee or their delegates. Accepted papers will be published on the workshop website.
